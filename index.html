Vladd

Fixed-precision editing 'desk' calculator

vladd is a fixed-precision editing calculator, not unlike Thomas Dickey's add, but graphical. It's a bit like one of those old-fashioned printing calculators.

Because vladd keeps intermediate results, you can correct mistakes made much earlier instead of having to re-enter the entire calculation.

It supports addition, substraction, multiplication, division, and power, and it has some nifty vi-like keys.
